# Community and Governance Suggestions

## Public Roadmap
- Keep a transparent roadmap with short/medium/long-term tracks.

## RFC Process
- Lightweight proposal process for major language/runtime changes.

## Labels and Triage
- Clear issue labels, triage rotation, and newcomer-friendly tags.

## Contributor Recognition
- Highlight contributors in releases and docs.

## Code of Conduct
- Maintain and enforce inclusive participation standards.

## Discussion Cadence
- Regular community threads for priorities and feedback.
